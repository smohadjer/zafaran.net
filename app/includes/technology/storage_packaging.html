Sulphur Storage and Handling

Overview

Zafaran provides all equipment and services required to handling, storing, loading, unloading and packing sulphur granules and pastils for sulphur solidification, sulphur forming and sulphur bentonite unit including:

  • Sulphur handling system such as rubber belt conveyor and bucket elevator
  • Silos with charging system
  • Truck loading system
  • Stacker and reclaimer
  • Weighing and bagging system
