Persian Gulf Star Refinery

This refinery located on west side of the existing refinery (25 kilometers from Bandar Abbas) at a 700 Hectares area. The goals of this project are refining 360,000 barrels per day of South Pars gas condensate, producing oil products according to Euro Standard, access to higher added value towards condensate gas production in South Pars refineries, and preventing the export of raw materials and changing into more valuable products. The final products are Liquid Gas (LPG), Petrol, Jet Fuel, Light Gasoline, Hydrogen and Granulated Sulphur.
