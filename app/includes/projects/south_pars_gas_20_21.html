South Pars Gas Field Development - Phases 20 & 21 - SSU

Phases 20 and 21 development was awarded to OIEC. These phases will produce 50 million cubic meters per day Natural Gas, 80,000 barrels per day Stabilized Condensate, 1 million tonnes per year LPG, 1 million tonnes per year Ethane Gas and 415 tonnes per day Sulphur.
