Sulphur Bentonite Plant

Sulphur is vital for life, and essential for plant growth. Like nitrogen, phosphorus and potassium, Sulphur is one of the nutrients essential to plant life. It contributes to higher crop yields and quality in different ways like: Provides a direct nutritional value, Improves the efficiency of other essential nutrient to plant, particularly N, P and some micronutrients, like Zn, Fe, Cu, Mn and B, Improves crop production quality by increasing the percentage of protein and oil in seeds, cereal quality for milling and baking, nutritional value and marketability of vegetables and fruits, quality of tobacco, nutritive value of forages,â€¦ etc.

In general, Sulphur has similar functions in plant growth and nutrition to N and plant requirements for Sulphur are comparable to P. This Sulphur Bentonite Plant is producing premium quality elemental Sulphur fertilizer and export to different countries.
