Tehran Oil Refinery

National Iranian Oil Engineering & Construction-NIOEC assigned Ehsan Joint Venture as EPC contractor of this project. The EPC Contractor (EHSAN joint venture) consisting of ODCC, IGC, and Design & Inspection Engineering Co. The Project scope consisting of Licensed and Non-licensed units is as follows: 53,500 bpd Gas oil Desulfurization Unit, 28,000 bpd Kerosene Desulfurization Unit, 42 million CFD Hydrogen Plant, 35 MW Power Generation Plant, LPG Recovery, Sour Water Unit, Amine Treatment Unit, Sulfur Recovery Unit and Sulphur Solidification Unit.

Sulphur Forming and Solidification Unit

Method Granulation
Client Ehsan J.V. (OD&CC, IGC, D&I)
